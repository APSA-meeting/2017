When can I interact with senior scientists?

Anytime! We have facilitated mentorship time at the mentorship breakfast, interest group breakfast, and residency luncheon. However, any time that you run across a senior scientist is an opportunity to interact. For example, the coffee breaks are a great time to do so. Just start by saying hi!
